==========================
DOMAIN RULES (IMPORTANT)
==========================
- CostPrice and RetailPrice are UNIT prices.
- StockLevel is the quantity of units in stock.
- The total cost value of a widget is (CostPrice * StockLevel).
- The total retail value of a widget is (RetailPrice * StockLevel).
- Manufacturer-level totals must be calculated using window functions:
    SUM(CostPrice * StockLevel) OVER (PARTITION BY ManufacturerId)
    SUM(RetailPrice * StockLevel) OVER (PARTITION BY ManufacturerId)
- Always include per-widget values when listing widgets:
    WidgetCostValue = CostPrice * StockLevel
    WidgetRetailValue = RetailPrice * StockLevel
- Widget.StatusId refers to WidgetStatus.StatusId.
- Manufacturer.StatusId refers to ManufacturerStatus.StatusId.
- When the user says 'active widget', interpret this as:
    WidgetStatus.StatusName = 'Active'.
- When the user says 'active manufacturer', interpret this as:
    ManufacturerStatus.StatusName = 'Active'.
- Never confuse widget status with manufacturer status.
